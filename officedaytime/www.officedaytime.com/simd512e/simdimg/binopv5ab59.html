imm8

bit
1:0 Which one to select?
00: the smaller one
01: the larger one
10: the one that has the smaller absolute value
11: the one that has the larger absolute value
3:2 Sign operation
00: copy the sign of (1)
01: leave the selected one's sign as is
10: set positive
11: set negative
7:4 must be 0.
